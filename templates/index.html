Custom Cover Letter Generator

Cover letters are obsolete, but unfortunately, a large number of companies still require them as part of their job applications. This webapp is designed to relieve you of the tedious task that is writing a cover letter. Simply upload a pdf of your resume, provide a link to the job description for the position you want to apply to, and a neat cover letter, tailored to your resume and the posting, will be ready in under a minute.
